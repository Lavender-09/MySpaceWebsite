What is a Natural Satellite?

A natural satellite is a celestial body that orbits a planet or another larger body in space.

üìå Did You Know?

  • A full moon occurs once every 29.5 days and a Blue Moon (the second full moon in a calendar month) occurs once every 2.5 years
  • A yellow moon is a phenomenon where the moon appears yellow due to atmospheric conditions
  • Earth's moon is the second densest satellite, the first being Io (Jupiter's moon)
  • The moon is in a synchronous rotation with Earth, meaning it always shows the same face to Earth
