ü™ê Characteristics

Venus is a small, rocky planet with a very thick atmosphere and very hot surface due to the greenhouse effect.

  • Atmosphere: Very thick, composed mainly of carbon dioxide, nitrogen, and sulfuric acid clouds
  • Surface: Rocky, dusty, iron-rich
  • Color: Bright white to yellowish, due to thick atmosphere and reflective clouds
  • Special Features: Cratered surface from impact craters, ancient lava flows and cliffs
  • Moons: None
  • Core Composition: Rich in iron and nickel, making up about 25% of the planet's mass
