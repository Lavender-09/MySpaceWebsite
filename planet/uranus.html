ü™ê Characteristics

Uranus is a gas giant with a thick atmosphere composed mainly of hydrogen, helium, and methane.

  • Atmosphere: Very thick, composed mainly of hydrogen, helium, and methane
  • Surface: No solid surface
  • Color: Blue-green due to methane in the atmosphere
  • Special Features: Icy rings, fast winds and surface that absorbs all red light
  • Moons: 29
  • Core Composition: Small, dense core composed of silicates and metals
