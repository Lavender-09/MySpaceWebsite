Venus is a small, rocky planet with a very thick atmosphere and very hot surface due to the greenhouse effect.

  • Atmosphere: Thin atmosphere made up of mostly carbon dioxide, nitrogen and argon gases
  • Surface: Rocky, dusty, iron-rich
  • Color: Mostly red due to iron oxide (rust) on the surface
  • Special Features: Inactive volcanoes, large canyon systems, Polar ice caps and salty water
  • Moons: Two (Phobos and Deimos)
  • Core Composition: Iron and nickel rich, making up about 25% of the planet's mass
