ü™ê Characteristics

Saturn is a gas giant with a thick atmosphere composed mainly of hydrogen and helium, and it has a prominent ring system.

  • Atmosphere: Thick, composed mainly of hydrogen and helium
  • Surface: No solid surface; the atmosphere gradually becomes denser
  • Color: Pale yellow to golden, due to ammonia crystals in the upper atmosphere
  • Special Features: Prominent ring system made of ice and rock particles
  • Moons: 146, making it the planet with the most known moons
  • Core Composition: Dense "fuzzy" region composed of silicates, metals and icy materials
